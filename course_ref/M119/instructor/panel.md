# Course Student Panel

## Description

The panel will be used to gain an understanding of data science student interaction with M112. 
The students will be DS majors or minors that have taken M112 and have participated in DS courses through their junior year.

## Questions

- Could you describe how you see calculus relates to statistics or data science?
- Based on the classes you have taken in the Math department, what structure of class have you thrived in the most?
- What is the difference between Mathematics and Statistics?
- What is the difference between Statistics and Data Science?
- If you were forced to take a Mathematics course (not statistics in the Math department) what would you want to understand from the course?
- If you could use programming as a key tool in your mathematics courses, do you think that would help or hurt your learning?
- What elements could we add to a new calculus course that would help you use your calculus training as a data scientist?
