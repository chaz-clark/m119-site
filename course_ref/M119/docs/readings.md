### Probability and Random Variables

#### Statistical Inference for Data Science

Brian Caffo published ['Statistical inference for data science'](https://leanpub.com/LittleInferenceBook). There are a few chapters that we will reference.

- **Chapter 2:** [Probability](https://leanpub.com/LittleInferenceBook/read#leanpub-auto-probability)
- **Chapter 4:** [Expected Values](https://leanpub.com/LittleInferenceBook/read#leanpub-auto-expected-values)
- **Chapter 5:** [Variation](https://leanpub.com/LittleInferenceBook/read#leanpub-auto-variation)
- **Chapter 6:** [Some common distributions](https://leanpub.com/LittleInferenceBook/read#leanpub-auto-some-common-distributions)
- **Chapter 7:** [Asymptopia](https://leanpub.com/LittleInferenceBook/read#leanpub-auto-asymptopia)
- **Chapter 10:** [P-values](https://leanpub.com/LittleInferenceBook/read#leanpub-auto-p-values)

#### Foundations of Statistics with R

Darrin Speegle and Bryan Clair have published a free web book titled ['Foundations of Statistics with R'](https://mathstat.slu.edu/~speegle/_book/index.html).  There are three chapters that relate to our course.

- **Chapter 2:** [Probability](https://mathstat.slu.edu/~speegle/_book/prob.html) 
- **Chapter 3:** [Random Variables](https://mathstat.slu.edu/~speegle/_book/random-variables.html)
- **Chapter 4:** [Simulation of Random Variables](https://mathstat.slu.edu/~speegle/_book/SimulationRV.html)
